Matt Davis–Dipsea–Steep Ravine Loop

Moderate · Loop

⭐ 3 ecosystems + ocean views + Steep Ravine ladder. THE classic loop.

📏 Distance6.8 mi
⛰️ Elevation~1,650 ft
🕐 Time3–4 hours
🌡️ Temp (Jul)55–70°F (fog near coast, sun up top)
💧 Water (Jul)Low — Webb Creek trickle in Steep Ravine; carry your own.
🅿️ ParkingPaid lot (~$8, cards OK biz hrs). Fills by 9am wknds.

Start A — Pantoll (paid lot · descend-first · ends shaded)
📍 37.90374, -122.60374 · directions ↗

Start B — Stinson Beach (FREE lot · climb-first · beach finish; fills by ~9am)
📍 37.89795, -122.64197 · directions ↗

🚗 After the hike → drive to East Peak (~15–20 min from Pantoll). The loop is coast-facing; the summit adds the 360° bay + city view it lacks — often above the July fog in the afternoon. Paved Verna Dunshee loop; cash/check parking. drive ↗

Dipsea Trail: Mill Valley to Stinson Beach

Strenuous · Point-to-point

⭐ 688 stairs, Cardiac Hill, ocean finish. Historic race route.

📏 Distance7.5 mi
⛰️ Elevation~2,200 ft
🕐 Time4-6 hours hiking
🌡️ Temp (Jul)60–75°F (warm inland, cooler at coast)
💧 Water (Jul)Mostly dry in July; none reliable — carry 2L+.
🅿️ ParkingMetered street parking; arrive early or transit.

📍 Start: Old Mill Park, Mill Valley · 37.90604, -122.54498 · directions ↗

Cataract Falls via Cataract Trail

Moderate · Out-and-back

⭐ Mile-long waterfall staircase — but only Dec–Apr.

📏 Distance6.8 mi
⛰️ Elevation~1,300 ft
🕐 Time3.5-4.5 hours
🌡️ Temp (Jul)62–78°F (exposed sections warm)
💧 Water (Jul)DRY in July ⚠ — waterfall needs winter/spring rain (peak Feb–Mar).
🅿️ ParkingFree dirt lot; fills early on wknds.

📍 Start: Rock Spring Trailhead · 37.91089, -122.61253 · directions ↗

Mt Tamalpais East Peak: Verna Dunshee Loop + Plankwalk Trail to Gardner Lookout

Easy · Loop

🚗 Drive-up ⭐ 2,571 ft summit, 360° views, fire lookout. Best per-mile payoff.

📏 Distance1.4 mi
⛰️ Elevation~350 ft
🕐 Time1 to 1.5 hours
🌡️ Temp (Jul)60–80°F, breezy; often ABOVE the fog
💧 Water (Jul)None on trail — bring your own. Fountains at lot.
🅿️ ParkingPaid lot — CASH/CHECK only (no cards).

📍 Start: East Peak parking lot · 37.92904, -122.57772 · directions ↗
